Clarion’s Role

Since civilization began, people have sought the means of organizing themselves into groups—so bigger things could be accomplished and so that a better-organized group of people couldn't subjugate them. In communities, towns, HOAs, universities—people are often unsatisfied with the quality of their representation. We aim to better serve the individuals in these communities.
