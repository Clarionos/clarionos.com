Our Dents in the Universe

We have a mission to fulfill, so we're moving fast toward that vision.
  • More Equal Animals: The book that elucidates the failings of election processes and proposes a new system of election and self-governance called Fractal Governance — tournament-style elections that result in delegates transparently and accountably spending budgets the community has entrusted to them.
  • EdenOS, the software that empowers communities to self-govern, following the process of Fractal Governance, as outlined in More Equal Animals.
  • Eden, the first community to experiment with and operate by the principles of Fractal Governance.
  • Technologies that make EOS blockchain apps easier to use. Clarion is at work on a pair of technologies "Contract pays" and "Server pays" that will eliminate one of the biggest obstacles to transacting on blockchain: transaction costs.
  • Technologies that make it easier to develop apps for the EOS blockchain. Clarion has made querying historical blockchain data simpler than ever before with its subchain technology. We have also made testing and debugging apps easier with a new testing library and the first EOS contract debugger.
  • Clarion continues to "dog food" its technologies by building the tools we need for EdenOS to work well and be highly performant. We will continue to build the tools that matter for writing apps and share them, as always, as open source software for all to leverage.
